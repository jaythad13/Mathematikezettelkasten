A property $P$ of a ring, if it is stable under [[Commutative algebra --- math-189AA/notes/Localisation of a ring#_definition _ localisation of a ring, localisations at a prime, fraction field|localisation]] (at a prime). That is, if $A$ has property $P$, then $A_{\mathfrak{p}}$ has property $P$ for all $\mathfrak{p} \in \operatorname{Spec} A$.
