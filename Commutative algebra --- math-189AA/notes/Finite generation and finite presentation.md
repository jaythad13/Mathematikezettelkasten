##### _definition:_ finitely presented

A module is **finitely presented** if there is an exact sequence
```tikz
\usepackage{tikz-cd}
\usepackage{amsfonts}
\begin{document}
	\begin{tikzcd}
		A^{\oplus m} \ar[r] & A^{\oplus n} \ar[r, "\varepsilon"] & M \ar[r] & 0
	\end{tikzcd}
\end{document}
```
