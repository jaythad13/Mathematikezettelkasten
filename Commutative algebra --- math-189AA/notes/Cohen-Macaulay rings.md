##### _definition:_ Cohen-Macaulay rings

A Noetherian local ring is **Cohen-Macaulay** if its [[Commutative algebra --- math-189AA/notes/Depth#_definition _ depth|depth]] over itself is the same as its Krull dimension.

---

##### _example:_ power series are Cohen-Macaulay

$k[[x, y]]$ is Cohen-Macaulay since $(0) \subseteq (x) \subseteq (x, y)$ is a maximal chain of prime ideals and $x, y$ is a maximal regular sequence on it.
