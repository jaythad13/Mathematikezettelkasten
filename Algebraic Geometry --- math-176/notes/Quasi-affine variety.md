We are going to completely redefine what affine $n$-space and [[Algebraic Geometry --- math-176/notes/Non-singular projective varieties#_definition _ affine variety|affine varieties]].

##### _definition:_ affine $n$-space

We define the affine $n$-space over a field $F$ to be $\mathbb{A}^n_{F} = \operatorname{Spec} F[x_{1}, \dots, x_{n}]$.

##### _definition:_ affine variety

An affine variety is a subset $X \subset \mathbb{A}^n_{F}$ with $X = \operatorname{Spec} \mathcal{O}$ for some $\mathcal{O} = F[x_{1}, \dots, x_{n}] / I$ for some prime ideal $I \in \mathbb{A}_{F}^n$.

##### _definition:_ dimension

$X$ has dimension given by the Krull dimension of its coordinate ring — $\dim X = \dim \mathcal{O}$.
