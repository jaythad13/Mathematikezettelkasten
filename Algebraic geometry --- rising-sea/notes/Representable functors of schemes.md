The yoga of representable functors takes [[Algebraic geometry --- rising-sea/notes/Yoneda's lemma|Yoneda's lemma]] and turns it into a definition. That is, if a scheme $X$ is uniquely determined by the functor of points $S \mapsto X(S)$, then we can just specify the functor of points.
