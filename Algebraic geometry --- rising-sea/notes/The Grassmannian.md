The Grassmannian, like [[Algebraic geometry --- rising-sea/notes/Examples of schemes#The projective line and projective space|projective space]] is a fundamental object of algebraic geometry, parameterising $k$-planes in an $n$-dimensional space. We will give increasingly sophisticated descriptions of the Grassmannian as we develop the machinery to state them.
