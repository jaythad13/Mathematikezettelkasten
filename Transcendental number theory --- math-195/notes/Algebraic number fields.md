Algebraic number fields are finite [[p-adic numbers --- math-177/notes/Algebraic field extensions#_definition _ algebraic, algebraic field extension, $ mathbb{F}[ alpha]$, $ mathbb{F}( alpha)$|algebraic field extensions]] of $\mathbb{Q}$. We restrict our attention to finite extensions, since they are always algebraic and are much nicer.
